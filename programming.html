Space Live Messenger

Space Live Messenger is a simulated network for us to explore the outer space. It can imitate the conversations between us and aliens on different planets, we can see aliens on web cam and hold simple conversations with them. We can also play a mini game with the aliens, just like what we do on MSN in the old days. We combine programming and animation to create the work, by using our own 3D designs of the planets, we can explore the universe in first person perspective, feeling more engaged in the virtual world. The interaction inside the planets can also enrich the experiences of the exploration.

Responsible for all programming and Maya modeling and animation.

We are always living a life with repetitive schedule and we gradually allow fewer and fewer space for us to think freely, irrationally and to imagine. In this project called 2015 Space Odyssey, we try to create a virtual space for people to explore. There would be different strange rooms in the virtual universe. There is not much instructions, people will find out what is interesting in the rooms when they try to explore different functions of the sensors such as tilt, controlling with joystick as well as the distance with the ultrasonic sensor. The settings were irrational and imaginative in the rooms yet they always come from some subjects in daily life. It is like a rhapsody in daily life, too have fun and free ourselves in the virtual world. To explore freely, to think freely.
