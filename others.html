“Bring Home the Bacon” is a relaxing board game to play with your family and friends. With a game play of 2-4 people, grill a set of meat and take them back to your plates safe! Enjoy the excitement of burning or stealing others’ meat. Protect your meat and be the most successful cook in the barbecue party! You are required to grill an assigned set of meat. Use different function cards to burn or steal others' meat, protect your meat and speed up the grilling time to finish the set as fast as possible. Use your cards wisely, plan your strategy and enjoy the game!

Responsible for idea development, game prototyping and game play design.
